About Becker Design

Becker Design is a design office opened in 2003 in Pasadena, California.  We offer a full line of residential design services including field survey work, design development, construction drawings, building permit acquisition and construction administration.  

William (Bill) Becker is a native of Seattle, Washington and a 1977 graduate of Washington State University.  A Pasadena resident for 25 years, Bill brings over 35 years of experience in design of structures and projects including:
  • single-family residences
  • residential remodels and additions
References are available upon request.
